I work on trustworthy NLG. More specifically, I build controlled text generation models and enhance the robustness of NLG systems, including NLG evaluation and fairness. More specifically:

  • Better Generation Evaluation: Dialect-Robust Evaluation [In Submission]; Evaluate the Helpfulness of Dialogue Responses [In Submission]; Robustness of Bias Evaluation [EMNLP 2022]
  • Generation Models: Paraphrase Generation [EMNLP 2021]; Controlled Pun Generation [EMNLP 2022]
  • Data Curation and Collection: Pun with Explanation [EMNLP 2022]; Investigate the Benefits of Free-form Rationales [EMNLP 2022]
I have also been actively working on Large Language Models (LLMs): 1) I pretrained models with over 60 TB data and then finetuneed them with mT5, ranging from mT5 base towards mT5 XXL (13 billion parameters). 2) I distill PaLM's ability of dialect rewriting into a smaller model based on EdiT5. Besides, I spent time working on fairness problems and promoting fair AI techniques, including event bias on Wikipedia [Best Paper Nomination @ ACL 2021] and on greeting cards [Best Paper Honorable Mention @ CHI 2022].
